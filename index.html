// About the Department

Profile & Overview

⚙️ Engineering Excellence Since 1983

The Department of Mechanical Engineering at Sanjivani College of Engineering, Kopargaon is one of the most distinguished departments in Maharashtra. Established in 1983, the department is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) and approved by AICTE.

The department offers a B.Tech (4-year) programme in Mechanical Engineering with an annual intake of 120 students. It also offers M.Tech programmes in Mechanical Design Engineering, Heat Power Engineering, and Production Engineering.

With state-of-the-art 12 laboratories, a dedicated Project Lab, and active participation in national-level competitions like SAE BAJA, the department prepares students to be industry-ready innovators.

The department houses an NBA-accredited programme and is committed to outcome-based education, focusing on developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and entrepreneurial skills.

About the Project

This website is developed as a part of the Continuous Internal Assessment (CIA) activity under the course "IT For Engineers (ESIT119)" by the students of First Year Mechanical Engineering, Department of Mechanical Engineering, Sanjivani College of Engineering, Kopargaon, under Sanjivani Rural Educational Society's, for the Academic Year 2025–26.

The project is created for educational and learning purposes using web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The objective of this activity is to enhance technical knowledge, creativity, and practical web development skills through engineering-based applications and digital presentation.

📚 Academics — Subjects & Assessment

Each subject follows the SPPU assessment pattern: 2 CIA (Continuous Internal Assessment) Tests, 1 End-Semester Examination, Lab Exam (where applicable), and Group Activity.

ASSESSMENT LEGEND

CIA-1 & CIA-2 End-Sem Exam Lab Exam Group Activity

CIA tests are conducted mid-semester by the department. End-Semester Exams are university-level. Lab exams assess practical skills. Group Activities involve collaborative project/presentation tasks.

🏆 Student Activities & Events

🚗 SAE BAJA / Sanjivani 4.0

SAEINDIA Sanjivani Collegiate Club (est. 2000) is the premier student engineering club of the Mechanical Department. Every year, student teams design, fabricate, and race All-Terrain Vehicles (ATVs) at the national BAJA SAEINDIA event — one of India's most prestigious engineering design competitions.

The Sanjivani 4.0 event is the department's flagship technical festival — a hackathon and competition platform that brings together students for design challenges, technical quizzes, project exhibitions, and innovation showcases. Teams from across Maharashtra participate in this grand celebration of mechanical engineering.

  • Indian Solar Vehicle Championship — Team Centaurus 2.0 secured AIR 1st Prize (₹1.15 Lakh)
  • National Electric Cart Championship — AIR 2nd position
  • E-BAJA 18 — Team Hawks secured AIR 8th
  • Annual participation in M-BAJA, E-BAJA, and TIFAN competitions
  • FSDC — Future Solar Design Challenge Championship 2019 participation

🚲 SAE BDC — Bicycle Design Competition

The SAE Bicycle Design Competition (BDC) challenges students to engineer high-performance, ergonomically designed cycles from scratch. This annual event tests knowledge of kinematics, material science, structural design, and manufacturing processes. Students compete for innovation, lightweight design, speed, and cost-efficiency.

Student teams at Sanjivani COE have consistently performed well in this event, fabricating competitive bicycles using advanced CAD/CAM techniques, finite element analysis, and precision machining in the department's well-equipped Project Lab.

  • Multi-stage competition: Design Report → Virtual Evaluation → Physical Event
  • Focuses on real-world engineering: ergonomics, material selection, manufacturing
  • Sanjivani teams have competed and won recognition at state and national level
  • Hands-on experience in welding, fabrication, 3D printing, and CNC machining
